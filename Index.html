Solace Rosemarie


TUMBLR INSTAGRAM


19 year old LLB Law with Politics student.

I use artistic media as a creative outlet to demonstrate a provocative and personalised perspective on the world around me. My influences are rooted in political debate and controversy, in addition to major themes which have exhibited significance throughout my upbringing and current lifestyle. I enjoy using an array of different materials and techniques and consistently investigate new methods to represent my voice through imaginative and sensory designs.

I seek to appreciate the detail, delicacy and elegance in subjects which are commonly interpreted as unsightly or grotesque. I use photography as a method of collating intriguing environmental aspects; consequently manipulating the recorded forms to inspire new projects. I tend to view existence through a nightmarish and hallucinatory lens and believe this reflects onto my macabre styling and construction. Be that as is may, I intend my work to be contributory to my pursuit of idealism, exposing weakness in a confrontational but cloudless manner.
